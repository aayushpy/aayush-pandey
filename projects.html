Ayurxiv (2023-)

ayurxiv.org

AyuRxiv (pronounced ‘ayur-archive’) is an open-access preprint repository for research papers in the fields of Ayurveda and its medical applications. By providing a platform for free and unrestricted access to high-quality preprints, AyuRxiv aims to accelerate the pace of scientific discovery, support evidence-based practices, and enhance the global visibility of Ayurvedic research.

Researcher, Prof. Ranjan Singh Group Nanyang Technological University (2023-)

TeraX Labs, Ranjan Singh Group

I am a researcher at the Ranjan Singh Group in the School of Mathematical and Physical Sciences (SPMS) at NTU. My current work involves novel applications of Terahertz (THz) Spectroscopy, such as in food and medical sciences.
