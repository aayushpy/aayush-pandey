May 2024: STEMonWHEELS, a social initiative in my home Uttarakhand, has recently been launched! The mission is simple: to democratize a STEM education in the mountanois villages of Uttarakhand through a mobile laboratory, much like other STEMonWHEELS projects around the world. However, this notice is also a call for volunteers. If you love teaching and the mountains - much like I do - please don't hesitate to drop an e-mail at axpandey[at]outlook[dot]com. If you love one but not the other, I promise you'll get used to it!
